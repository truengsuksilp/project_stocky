    What is Stocky?

    Stocky is your power tool to maximize your existing stock portfolio to maximizes return over risk, i.e. portfolio optimization

    ​

    Every investor wants to maximize return over risk, but the tools are not accessible or usable.  Stocky is power tool to create a personalized map of possible investment positions based on expected return and risk of companies you invest in.

    ​

    How it works?

    Stocky uses powerful analytical frameworks including Modern Portfolio Theory and Monte Carlo simulations.  This generates thousands of possible outcomes for your portfolio #PortfolioVisualization. 

     

    The tool then searches for optimal outcomes and provide recommend solutions
